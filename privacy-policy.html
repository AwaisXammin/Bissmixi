01.Introduction

BiSSMiXi respects the privacy, confidentiality, and security of all users, visitors, collaborators, clients, and individuals interacting with its website, social media platforms, digital channels, affiliate links, communications, and related services.

This Privacy Policy outlines how BiSSMiXi may collect, use, process, store, manage, disclose, protect, and handle personal information and data obtained through interactions with the brand and its platforms.

By accessing, browsing, using, communicating with, or interacting with BiSSMiXi in any manner, users acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to the practices described within this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Website visitors

  • Clients and collaborators
  • Brands and service providers

  • Individuals submitting forms, inquiries, subscriptions, or communications

This Policy applies across all digital channels, services, communications, and operational activities associated with BiSSMiXi unless otherwise stated.

Submitted Materials
